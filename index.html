Who I Am

Analyst brain.
Maker hands.

I've spent 19 years finding patterns — in data, in systems, in the way things work. But the brain moves faster than spreadsheets allow, so I build to keep up.

From FPV drones assembled from raw parts to data pipelines that reveal what no one else noticed — I'm most alive when I'm making something real from nothing.

I'm drawn to water, forests, and waterfalls. I read the Bhagavad Gita. I lift weights. I believe the best ideas come from people who stay curious and keep showing up.

Right now I'm teaching myself to ship software — building products, launching sites, learning what it means to go from idea to live URL. This site is proof of that.
